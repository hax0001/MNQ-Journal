MNQ DAY TRADER JOURNAL — OFFLINE EDITION
========================================

✅ 100% local — no internet, no login, no cloud
✅ Built for 1 trade/day — visual + data
✅ Saves to localStorage (cleared only if you clear browser data)
✅ Export Excel monthly

HOW TO USE:
1. Extract this folder anywhere on your laptop.
2. Open "index.html" in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
3. Start logging your one daily MNQ trade.

TIPS:
- Use "Add Trade" to log your setup with screenshot.
- Dashboard updates automatically.
- "Analyses" tab shows your edge visually.
- Export Excel at month-end for backup.

All data stays on your machine.

Trade well. Journal better.
